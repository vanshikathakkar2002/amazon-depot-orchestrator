01 — Problem
Five Gaps Blocking Amazon's EV Scale
⚡

Limited Charging Infrastructure

Depots lack sufficient chargers and grid capacity, delaying fleet electrification and limiting EV deployment at scale across 1,800 sites.

🔧

Manual Coordination

Static charging schedules leave vans undercharged at shift start. Manual routing creates delays, high costs, and operational errors daily.

🚐

Congestion & Inefficient Yard Flow

Vans idle and queue, blocking bays and slowing yard flow — reducing charger utilization and delaying morning dispatch windows.

💰

High Energy & Grid Costs

Peak-hour charging drives volatile energy spend. Without load intelligence, EV economics weaken and grid upgrade costs spike.

🧩

Fragmented Systems

Routing, charging, and scheduling systems don't talk to each other. No central visibility means missed performance goals at scale.

🎯

The Core Question

How can Amazon scale its EV fleet while achieving depot efficiency and net-zero carbon emissions by 2040 — across 10,000+ vehicles in 1,800 cities?

Evaluated 5 core AI technology areas for feasibility, deployment readiness, and business impact — mapped to specific operational pain points across the AMZL depot ecosystem. Identified proven vendor solutions, benchmarked results, and industry tailwinds supporting immediate deployment.

⚡ Smart EMS

Minimize energy cost and avoid grid spikes. ML-driven scheduling avoids peak tariffs, prevents power spikes. Deploy: Ampcontrol AI or Enel X JuiceNet. Proven: 30–40% energy cost savings.

🗺 Route + Charge AI

Match state-of-charge with route urgency. AI dynamically plans most efficient routes nightly using traffic, battery data, and weather. Deploy: Google Fleet Routing API. Proven: UPS ORION saves 100M+ miles/year.

🅿 Yard Flow Simulation

Avoid congestion, assign stalls optimally. Simulation forecasts van movements, assigns vehicles to optimal stalls. Deploy: AnyLogic or Simio. Proven: DHL 20% congestion reduction.

🔋 Battery AI

Protect from fast-charging stress. Neural networks track battery health patterns, prevent overcharging and high-SoC cycles, extending battery life 15%+.

👥 Shift Interface

Real-time handoffs for drivers and depot managers. Driver app: charging overview, dynamic route dashboard, shift handoff. Manager app: live occupancy, congestion forecast, stall planning.

📈 Industry Tailwinds

Zero-emission delivery mandates (US & EU), rising energy tariff complexity, mandatory ESG reporting, AI adoption in fleet ops by competitors, and SaaS monetization potential for 3rd-party DSPs.

RISKS & MITIGATIONS

Infrastructure Mismatch

Depot charger types and grid strengths differ. Mitigation: modular, adaptable design with configurable hardware abstraction layer.

Operational Resistance

AI introduces workflow changes. Mitigation: co-design pilot programs, human-in-the-loop design, gradual automation rollout.

Integration Complexity

Legacy systems are hard to connect. Mitigation: open APIs and middleware data links to reduce disruption to existing systems.

Model Drift & Data Governance

AI accuracy drops without retraining; real-time tracking raises GDPR/CCPA concerns. Mitigation: continuous monitoring, early compliance design, transparent data policies.

AI Solution 1 — Smart Charging

Predictive off-peak charging scheduler. Cuts energy costs 40%, peak-demand charges from $450K → $120K/month. Deploy: Ampcontrol AI or Enel X JuiceNet.

AI Solution 2 — Route Optimization

ML models using parcel volume, weather, battery SoC. Cuts last-mile costs 15%, improves on-time delivery 8–12%. Deploy: Google Fleet Routing API.

AI Solution 3 — Yard Orchestration

Simulation-based stall assignment and exit-flow management. Reduces depot congestion 20%, trailer movement efficiency +35%. Deploy: AnyLogic or Simio.

Driver App Features

Depot Manager App Features
